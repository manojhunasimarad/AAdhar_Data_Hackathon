> Raw CSV shards were consolidated and validated. State, district, and pincode fields were standardized. Exact duplicate rows were identified and removed to prevent artificial inflation of counts. Date formats were strictly validated.

### 3.2 Geographic Integrity Audits

> Cross-validation checks were performed to identify pincodes mapped to multiple districts or states. Such cases were flagged as geographic ambiguities rather than corrected automatically.
